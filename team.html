Our Team

The people behind Ripple

Purandhar Ram Choppa

Team Lead

Purandhar leads the Ripple team with a focus on community impact and overall project direction. He coordinates cross-functional efforts to ensure the platform delivers meaningful value to Forsyth County residents. Purandhar is passionate about using technology to connect people with the local resources they need.

Prayag Patel

Lead Developer

Prayag drives the technical development of Ripple, building the systems that power its events, calendar, and community features. He brings a detail-oriented approach to both backend logic and overall site architecture. Prayag is committed to creating a fast, reliable experience for every user who visits the platform.

Mihir Malladi

UI/UX Designer

Mihir shapes the look and feel of Ripple, ensuring every page is visually cohesive and easy to navigate. He translates user needs into clean, accessible designs that reflect the platform's community-forward mission. Mihir believes great design should be invisible — intuitive enough that it simply gets out of the way.

Advay Pradeep

Frontend Developer

Advay builds the interactive features that bring the Ripple interface to life, from responsive layouts to dynamic components. He focuses on ensuring the site feels smooth and polished across all screen sizes and devices. Advay enjoys the challenge of turning design concepts into fully working, production-ready code.

Saaketh Alla

Content & Research Lead

Saaketh leads content strategy and research for Ripple, sourcing the resources, events, and community groups featured on the platform. He ensures all information is accurate, well-organized, and genuinely useful for Forsyth County residents. Saaketh's work keeps Ripple grounded in the real needs of the local community it serves.
